★ Read the dialogues and check the bold phrases.
Scene 1: Victory Celebration
Dad
▶
Shelly, congratulations! Why don't we go to the steakhouse to celebrate your win?
Shelly
▶
I'm not sure. I eat a lot of meat for my muscles every day. I'm in the mood for pizza tonight.
Mom
▶
A new Italian restaurant opened nearby. They have great pizza. Shall we go?
Shelly
▶
That's a good idea! I'd love to! Dad, how about you?
Dad
▶
Of course! Let's go.

Waitress
▶
May I help you?
Dad
▶
We'd like to start off with some drinks.
Waitress
▶
Certainly!
Mom
▶
Can we have the drink menu?
Waitress
▶
Sure. Here you are.

Shelly
▶
Let's order two extra large pizzas so that we can share!
Mom
▶
No, thanks.
Shelly
▶
Why not?
Mom
▶
I'm just not in the mood for cheese today.
Dad
▶
How about the Marinara? It has no cheese.
Mom
▶
Well... but I really want to eat seafood today.
Waitress
▶
Are you ready to order?
Mom
▶
Shelly, go ahead and order your pizza.
Shelly
▶
Okay! I'll have the Margherita.
Dad
▶
And I'll have the Pepperoni.
Mom
▶
Waitress, what do you recommend for seafood?
Waitress
▶
The seafood pasta with fresh clams is excellent.
Mom
▶
That sounds good! I'll have that.

Shelly
▶
This pizza is amazing! I love it!
Dad
▶
Me, too. Oh! I dropped my fork.
Waitress
▶
Don't worry. I'll bring you another one right away.

▶
Oh, thank you.
Word Bank:
* clams: あさり、ハマグリなどの二枚貝
* Margherita / Marinara / Pepperoni: ピザの種類（マルゲリータ、マリナーラ、ペペロニ）
* in the mood for ~: ～をしたい気分だ
